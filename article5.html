Colic Symptoms Can Be Treated With Probiotics
Extracted from Probiotics for infantile colic: a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial investigating Lactobacillus reuteri DSM 17938, available at PubMed.gov

Colicky babies can make parenting a challenge. Colic is identified as pain and discomfort in the abdomen due to intestinal gas or obstructions, and it is most commonly found in babies. Colic is diagnosed in babies based on three general rules of crying.
